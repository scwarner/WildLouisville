About Bernheim Forest

Located a half hour south of Louisville in Clermont, Ky., Bernheim Arboretum and Research Forest offers a variety of hikes, from several easy short trails to the 13.75-mile Millennium Trail, along with educational programming for all ages. Hikers can find sustenance after their treks at the forest's cafe, and explore the beautiful visitor's center.
