Jefferson Memorial Forest

This 6,000-acre forest, located 15 miles southwest of downtown Louisville, affords hikers plenty of opportunities to trek along its 35 miles of trails. The park also offers an array of educational programs to residents, plus a ropes/zipline course. Its diverse trails are popular for trail runners and hikers alike.
