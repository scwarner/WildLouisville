Falls of the Ohio State Park

The fossils at the Falls of the Ohio State Park are easy to find.

Views of downtown Louisville remind Falls of the Ohio visitors just how close they are to the city.

Hiking at the Falls of the Ohio means climbing over rocky terrain. Sturdy shoes are a must.

In addition to hiking and fossil hunting, fishing is a popular activity at the park.

About Falls of the Ohio

Do you dream of being a fossil hunter? Then a visit to the Falls of the Ohio, across the river from Louisville in Clarksville, Ind., is for you. You can climb the rocks searching for fossils, but remember that the only thing you should take are pictures and memories! The park also has a recently renovated interpretive center where you can learn about the natural history of the area.

Things to Do
