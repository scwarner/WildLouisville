Cherokee Park

The many green spaces at Cherokee Park are appreciated by all types of outdoor enthusiasts.

Cherokee Park is set in the valley of Beargrass Creek.

About Cherokee Park

One of the many parks in Louisville designed by famed 19th-century landscape architect Frederick Law Olmsted, Cherokee has trails popular among hikers, mountain bikers and trail runners that make you forget you are in an urban setting. Its paved 2.4-mile scenic loop around the park is a haven for dog walkers, cyclists and runners.
